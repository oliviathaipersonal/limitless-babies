Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Limitless Babies (the "App") is an early-learning flashcard app for babies and toddlers, designed and operated by Olivia Thai Martinez ("we," "us"). We take your family's privacy seriously, and we've built the App to collect as little data as possible. This policy explains what data the App handles and how.

The short version

  • The App stores your children's profiles, progress, and uploaded family photos only on your device.
  • We do not run user accounts, we do not have a server that stores your data, and we do not sell or share your data with anyone.
  • We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking pixels in the App.
  • Subscription billing, when active, is handled by Apple's App Store — we never see your payment information.

What the App stores on your device

When you use Limitless Babies, the following information is saved to your phone or tablet's local storage and stays there:

  • Children's names, birthdates, languages, and avatar photos that you choose to add
  • Progress through the curriculum (which sets each child has completed, today's session count, etc.)
  • Family photos you upload to personalize your child's flashcards (mom, dad, grandma, your home, etc.)
  • App preferences (active language, voice overrides, etc.)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back up your phone using Apple's iCloud Backup feature, which is governed by Apple's privacy policy, not ours. If you delete the App, all of this data is removed from your device.

What the App does NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or location.
  • We do not collect your child's name, birthdate, or photos — those stay on your device only.
  • We do not use cookies, advertising IDs, or analytics tools.
  • We do not contact remote servers for content other than to load curriculum images from public CDNs (described below).

Network requests the App makes

The App loads curriculum flashcard images from publicly-available image CDNs (such as Pixabay, Unsplash, and iStock) when displaying lesson cards. These requests are standard image loads — your phone's IP address is visible to those CDNs, as it would be when loading any image on the web. We do not pass any information about you or your children in these requests.

Subscriptions and payments

When subscriptions are enabled, billing is processed entirely through Apple's App Store using In-App Purchase. We never see your credit card number, billing address, or full payment information. Apple shares limited subscription status (active / lapsed / canceled) with us so we can grant or revoke premium features. Apple's privacy policy applies to your purchase: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/en-ww/.

Children's privacy

Limitless Babies is designed for use by parents on behalf of their babies and toddlers. Because we do not collect any personal information from anyone — including children — there is nothing for us to store, share, or delete. If you discover that the App is collecting data we haven't disclosed here, please contact us immediately and we will investigate.

Voice and audio

The App uses your device's built-in text-to-speech engine (Apple's AVSpeechSynthesizer or the browser's Web Speech API) to read flashcards aloud. The text being spoken is processed entirely on your device by the operating system's voice engine. We do not record, transmit, or analyze any audio.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes (for example, if we ever begin using analytics) will be announced in the App and via the email address you registered with the App Store, if applicable.
