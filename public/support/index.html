Frequently asked questions

How do I add another child to my account?

From the home screen, tap your child's avatar at the top to open the child switcher, then tap "+ Add another child." Each child has their own profile, languages, and progress.

How do I switch the language for a session?

On the home screen, tap the flag chip below your child's name. You'll see all the languages enrolled for that child. Pick one and the next session will use it. You can change languages mid-day as often as you like.

Can I add a new language for my child?

Yes. From your child's profile, scroll to "Languages to learn" and tap "+ add language." We support 30+ languages with native pronunciation.

Why does my baby's session use a robotic voice instead of a native voice?

iOS includes a built-in voice for every supported language. For higher-quality voices in some languages (especially Cantonese, Wu, Korean, and a few others), iOS lets you download enhanced voices. To do this, open Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices, then pick a language and tap to download. Once downloaded, Limitless Babies will use it automatically.

How does the free trial work?

Limitless Babies offers a free 14-day trial of all premium features — no credit card required. You can use the entire app during the trial. After 14 days, you can choose to subscribe ($14.99/month or $119/year) or continue with the free tier.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple, not through us. To cancel:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top → Subscriptions
  3. Find Limitless Babies and tap Cancel Subscription

You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period. There are no cancellation fees.

How do I get a refund?

Apple handles all refunds for App Store subscriptions. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find Limitless Babies, and select "Request a refund." Apple typically responds within 48 hours.

I subscribed on another device — how do I unlock premium here?

Open the app, go to Parent Profile → tap "Restore Purchases." This pulls your subscription status from Apple and unlocks premium on this device. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

Can I share my account with my partner or another caregiver?

Yes. From your child's profile, tap "Share my family" to generate a code. Your partner or caregiver can enter that code on their own device to access the same children's profiles and progress.

How do I upload family photos?

Go to your child's profile → "Family Photos." You can add photos of mom, dad, grandma, your home, and other meaningful places. These photos will appear during relevant flashcard sessions, making the learning more personal and meaningful for your baby.

How do I delete my data?

All Limitless Babies data — children's profiles, progress, photos, preferences — is stored only on your device. To delete everything, simply uninstall the app. Nothing is stored on our servers.

Is Limitless Babies appropriate for my child's age?

The app is designed for babies and toddlers from roughly 3 months to 3 years. The Doman methodology behind it is most effective in the early years when the brain is most plastic. Older children can still benefit, especially for language learning.

How long should each session be?

Sessions are intentionally short — about 30 to 60 seconds — because that respects a baby's natural attention span. The app guides you through 3 sessions per day with breaks in between. More is not better; consistency is what matters.
